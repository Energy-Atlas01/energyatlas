Cascade Power Plant — Alberta, Canada

900 MW combined-cycle gas plant to supply over 8 % of Alberta’s electricity demand.
CountryCanada
RegionAlberta
IndustryGas (Combined-Cycle)
Company / OEMSiemens Energy + Kineticor
Capacity900 MW
StatusUnder Construction
Completion2023

The Cascade Power Plant is a significant infrastructure project driven by Siemens Energy and Kineticor. Located near Edson in Alberta, this 900 MW combined‑cycle gas‑fired plant aims to reduce reliance on coal and support decarbonisation. Siemens Energy will provide two SGT6‑8000H gas turbines and related equipment.

Once operational, the plant is expected to generate electricity equivalent to over eight percent of Alberta’s average power demand, creating approximately 600 peak jobs and 25 long‑term positions.
